City Walk 17.4.
True to tradition we will once again offer a free city walk to those arriving early, or who just happen to live nearby.
On Thursday April 17th we invite you to join us for a walk from Majorstua to Aker Brygge. The meetup point will be at Vinkelplassen at Majorstua by 11:00. We will depart shortly thereafter.
The first stop will be the world famous Frognerparken, with its sculpture park by Gustav Vigeland. We will spend a bit of time here taking a good look at the main part of the park.
From the park we will follow a little river down to Skøyen, a former industrial area turned into fancy office space. From there we will cross the highway and follow the waterfront past the Color Line cruise terminal to Aker Brygge, known for its architecture and expensive apartments.
This will lead us to our final stop, Via Village, where we will have lunch. The estimated time of arrival here is around 14:00.
Bring shoes you can walk in, water, and maybe a snack.
Fursuits are welcome, but no fursuit support is provided.
Using a wheelchair? Most of the trip is on paved roads, but the section from the park to Skøyen is on a dirt road with a couple steep moments.
Dead Dog Afternoon 19.4.
Need to chill and recover after the party? Join us for a late lunch/early dinner at Vippa Street Food on Saturday the 19th from 16:00 to 18:00. (Vippa's website)
Vippa has their own little Easter event going on and will be providing a live DJ, as well as a variety of food stalls.
We have booked table space enough for all and hope to see you there and to hear about what your Pawske was like.
This event is free and open for all, but you are expected to purchase something from the venue.
