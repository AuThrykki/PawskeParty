Welcome back to Pawske Party!
We are super excited to welcome you back for our third year, and to our brand new venue Quality Hotel Hasle Linie.
Three days of fun await you in Oslo, Norway. The main day is of course Easter Friday, which is April 18th next year, but things are happening both before and after.
On Thursday April 17th you can join us for a touristic city walk during the day, and our traditional early arrival bowling in the evening. Saturday the 19th sees the return of the Dead Dog Afternoon. More information about that later.
Pawske Party itself takes place on April 18th with various events and panels during the day, and the party in the evening. In between you can enjoy a delicious taco buffet for just 300 kr!
